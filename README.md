# Voidloop game

A new Flutter project.

## Getting Started
🪐 ORBIT — Hop. Dodge. Survive.

Can you navigate the cosmos one orbit at a time?

Orbit is a minimalist space game that's easy to pick up but nearly impossible to master. Your glowing spacecraft circles a planet, waiting for the perfect moment to break free and leap to the next orbit. Time your launch, control your speed, and thread the gap between deadly obstacles — all while the universe tries its hardest to pull you into the void.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
## HOW TO PLAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Your ship automatically orbits the current planet. When you're ready to jump:

① Tap and hold anywhere on screen
② Watch the power bar — it moves from Yellow to Green to Red
③ Release in the Green zone for the perfect launch
④ Your ship flies in the direction it was orbiting — aim by waiting for the right moment
⑤ Land on the next planet's orbit ring to score points and keep going

Simple controls. Infinite depth.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
## THE POWER BAR
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🟡 Yellow — Too slow. You might not reach the next orbit.
🟢 Green — Perfect speed. Clean landing guaranteed.
🔴 Red — Too fast. You'll overshoot and drift into danger.

The bar oscillates back and forth, so you always get another chance — but hesitate too long and the obstacles will close in.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
DANGERS OF DEEP SPACE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The cosmos is not empty. As you venture deeper into space, the threats multiply:

☄️ Asteroids — Rocky debris scattered freely between planets and orbiting dangerously close to your path. Some drift slowly, others circle at high speed. One touch and it's over.

🔴 Orbital Mines — Glowing red spheres locked in tight orbits around planets. They never stop moving. Find the gap, time your entry, and don't blink.

🌑 Black Holes — The most terrifying force in the universe. Black holes bend space around them, pulling your ship off course with invisible gravity. Stray too close and you'll spiral in with no escape. The closer you fly, the stronger the pull.

Every few orbits, a new threat appears. Every few levels, the gaps get tighter.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
## SCORING SYSTEM
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🏅 Land on the next orbit — earn points multiplied by your current level
🚀 Skip multiple orbits in one jump — earn bonus points for each orbit skipped
❌ Going backwards — no points awarded
⭐ The further you go, the higher the multiplier

Think you can play it safe? The game rewards boldness. Skip two orbits in one daring jump and watch your score multiply. But miss, and it's over.

## FEATURES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✦ Infinitely generated levels — no two runs are ever the same
✦ Smooth 60fps gameplay built with Flutter and Flame engine
✦ Progressive difficulty — obstacles increase in number and speed as you advance
✦ Local best score saved on your device — beat your personal record every run
✦ Pause anytime — the game freezes the moment you switch apps and waits for your return
✦ Portrait mode — perfect for one-handed play
✦ 100% offline — no internet required, no ads, no interruptions
✦ Vibrant deep space visuals — glowing planets, asteroid fields, black hole vortexes, and a star-filled cosmos
✦ Satisfying physics — orbits feel real, launches feel weighty, every close call feels earned
